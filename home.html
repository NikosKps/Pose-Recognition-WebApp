App Demo

What it does.

A browser-based AI tool that tracks your body posture in real time — no installation required.

Pose Detection
Tracks key body landmarks in real time using your webcam.
100% Private
All processing runs locally in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.
Instant Results
Live feedback with no delay. Accuracy score after a 10-second test.
15s Test Mode
Hold a pose for 15 seconds and get a confidence percentage score.

How to use it.

Get started in three simple steps.

01
Open the App Demo
Click the "App Demo" button in the top navbar to go to the mode selection screen.
02
Choose a mode
Live Tracking gives you continuous real-time pose recognition. 15s Posture Test records your posture for 15 seconds and returns an accuracy score.
03
Allow camera access & start
Press Start, allow your browser to access the camera, and step into frame. The AI will begin detecting your pose immediately.
