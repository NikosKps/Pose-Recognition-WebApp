SECTION 01 / WHAT

A trained model, running entirely on your device.

Real-time pose detection
Tracks 17 body landmarks every frame using your webcam, drawn directly onto the live canvas.
100% private
Inference runs locally in TensorFlow.js. No frames, no keypoints, no metadata leave the device.
Instant feedback
Confidence updates frame-by-frame. The 15-second test returns a full accuracy report under a second.
15-second test mode
Hold a pose for 15 seconds and receive a confidence percentage across 7 trained classes plus a movement log.

SECTION 03 / HOW

Three steps. No setup.

STEP 01
Open the demo
Hit Launch Demo from the navbar to enter the mode-selection screen.
STEP 02
Pick a mode
Live Tracking gives you continuous detection. The 15-second test scores you across the 7 trained classes.
STEP 03
Allow camera & start
Approve camera access, step into frame, and begin. Detection starts on the very first frame.
